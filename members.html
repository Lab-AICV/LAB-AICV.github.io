Director of the AICV Research Group

Mohammad Biglarbegian Ph.D., P.Eng

Professor Biglarbegian is the founder and director of the Autonomous & Intelligent Control for Vehicles lab at the University of Guelph. His research interests are design, control, and optimization of mechatronics sytstems especially for autonomous robots and cars. He and his students have been developing advanced algorithms for intelligent vehicles and advanced robotics.
