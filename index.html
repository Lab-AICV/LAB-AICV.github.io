Autonomous & Intelligent Control for Vehicles

The Autonomous & Intelligent Control for Vehicles Research Group focuses on design, control, and optimization of Mechatronics systems. The main theme of our research focuses on developing advanced control and optimization techniques for Mechatronics, particularly Robotics (mobile robots, reconfigurable robot manipulators) and Automotive (autonomous vehicles)
